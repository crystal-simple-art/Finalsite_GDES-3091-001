About Cantonese Cuisine
Cantonese cuisine, also called Yue cuisine or Guangdong cuisine—is one of the eight major cuisines in China with a long history of about 2,000 years. It is featured with widely use of materials and rich cooking methods. It is also the most widely served style of Chinese cuisine in the world.
